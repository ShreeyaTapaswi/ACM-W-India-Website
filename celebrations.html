Celebrations of ACM-W India

"ACM-W India's annual celebrations are not merely events — they are living testaments to the strides women in computing have made across India. We celebrate not just achievement, but the courage it takes to lead in a field that still needs more women."

— Dr. Geetanjali Kale, Chair, ACM-W India (2025–2027)

Annual Symposium

The ACM-W India Annual Symposium is the flagship gathering of the women-in-computing community in India, bringing together students, researchers, and industry professionals for keynotes, workshops, and networking.

1

Awards & Honours

Each year ACM-W India recognises outstanding women researchers, educators and student achievers through its awards programme.

Women's Day Events

On International Women's Day each March, ACM-W India chapters across the country host panel discussions, speaker series and outreach events to celebrate and inspire women in computing.

About Celebrations
